# Sales Order Creation

## Business Situation

Balaji Tiles & Sanitary wants to purchase 100 bags of Type 2 Tile Adhesive from BuildBond Chemicals Pvt. Ltd.

The customer places the order, and the sales team creates a Sales Order in SAP S/4HANA.

---

## Sales Order Details

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Order Type | OR - Standard Order |
| Sales Organization | BS01 |
| Distribution Channel | 10 - Dealer Sales |
| Division | 01 - Construction Chemicals |
| Sold-to Party | Balaji Tiles & Sanitary |
| Customer Reference | DEALER-ORDER-001 |
| Material | BB-T2-20KG |
| Product | Type 2 Tile Adhesive |
| Order Quantity | 100 Bags |
| Requested Delivery Date | 7 Days from Order Date |

---

## Simple Process

1. The dealer places an order for 100 bags.
2. The sales team enters the customer and product details in SAP.
3. SAP checks the order information.
4. The Sales Order is saved.
5. A Sales Order number is generated.

---

## Expected Result

A Standard Sales Order is successfully created for 100 bags of Type 2 Tile Adhesive.

The next step in the process is to create an Outbound Delivery.

---

## SAP Learning

This step demonstrates how a customer requirement is converted into a formal Sales Order in SAP S/4HANA Sales.

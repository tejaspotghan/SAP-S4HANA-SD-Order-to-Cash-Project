# Billing

## Business Situation

100 bags of Type 2 Tile Adhesive have been successfully dispatched to Balaji Tiles & Sanitary.

The company now needs to create an invoice for the customer.

---

## Billing Details

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Customer | Balaji Tiles & Sanitary |
| Reference Document | Outbound Delivery |
| Material | BB-T2-20KG |
| Product | Type 2 Tile Adhesive |
| Billed Quantity | 100 Bags |
| Currency | INR |
| Payment Terms | 30 Days |

---

## Simple Process

1. The goods are successfully dispatched to the customer.
2. The delivery becomes ready for billing.
3. A Billing Document is created with reference to the Outbound Delivery.
4. SAP copies the customer, material, and quantity information.
5. An invoice number is generated.
6. The customer receives the invoice.

---

## Expected Result

A Billing Document is successfully created for 100 bags of Type 2 Tile Adhesive.

The customer is now required to make payment according to the agreed payment terms.

---

## SAP Learning

This step demonstrates the final stage of the sales process in SAP S/4HANA Sales.

The Billing Document creates the customer invoice and connects the sales process with financial accounting.

---

## Complete Order-to-Cash Flow

**Customer Order → Sales Order → Outbound Delivery → Picking → Post Goods Issue → Billing**

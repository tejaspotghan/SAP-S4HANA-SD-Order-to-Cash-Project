# Business Scenario

## Company Overview

BuildBond Chemicals Pvt. Ltd. is a fictional construction chemicals company that sells tile adhesives, tile grout, and waterproofing products.

The company sells its products through distributors, dealers, and project customers.

## Business Situation

A dealer wants to purchase 100 bags of Type 2 Tile Adhesive.

The company needs to manage the complete customer order in SAP S/4HANA Sales, starting from the customer order and ending with billing.

## Process Covered

Customer Order → Sales Order → Outbound Delivery → Picking → Post Goods Issue → Billing

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to demonstrate how an end-to-end Order-to-Cash process can be executed in SAP S/4HANA Sales for a construction chemicals business.

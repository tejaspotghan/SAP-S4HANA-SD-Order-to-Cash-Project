# Picking and Post Goods Issue

## Business Situation

The Outbound Delivery for 100 bags of Type 2 Tile Adhesive has been created.

The warehouse now prepares the material and dispatches it to Balaji Tiles & Sanitary.

---

## Picking

Picking means selecting and preparing the ordered products from the warehouse.

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Customer | Balaji Tiles & Sanitary |
| Material | BB-T2-20KG |
| Product | Type 2 Tile Adhesive |
| Delivery Quantity | 100 Bags |
| Picking Quantity | 100 Bags |
| Plant | BP01 - Ahilyanagar Plant |

### Simple Picking Process

1. The warehouse receives the Outbound Delivery.
2. The warehouse finds the required material.
3. 100 bags are selected for the customer.
4. The Picking Quantity is confirmed.

---

## Post Goods Issue (PGI)

After picking is complete, the company dispatches the goods.

Post Goods Issue records in SAP that the material has officially left the company.

### What Happens After PGI?

1. The goods leave the warehouse.
2. The stock quantity is reduced in SAP.
3. The Outbound Delivery status is updated.
4. The process becomes ready for billing.

---

## Expected Result

100 bags of Type 2 Tile Adhesive are successfully picked and dispatched to the customer.

The next step is to create the Billing Document.

---

## SAP Learning

This step demonstrates how SAP connects the physical movement of goods with the system process.

Picking prepares the material for dispatch, while Post Goods Issue records that the goods have left the company's stock.

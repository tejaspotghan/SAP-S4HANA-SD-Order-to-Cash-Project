# Outbound Delivery

## Business Situation

The Sales Order for 100 bags of Type 2 Tile Adhesive has been created successfully.

The warehouse now needs to prepare the ordered material for delivery to Balaji Tiles & Sanitary.

---

## Delivery Details

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Reference Document | Sales Order |
| Customer | Balaji Tiles & Sanitary |
| Material | BB-T2-20KG |
| Product | Type 2 Tile Adhesive |
| Delivery Quantity | 100 Bags |
| Plant | BP01 - Ahilyanagar Plant |
| Shipping Point | SP01 - Ahilyanagar Dispatch Point |

---

## Simple Process

1. The Sales Order is ready for delivery.
2. An Outbound Delivery is created with reference to the Sales Order.
3. SAP copies the customer, material, and quantity from the Sales Order.
4. The warehouse receives the delivery information.
5. A Delivery Document number is generated.

---

## Expected Result

An Outbound Delivery is successfully created for 100 bags of Type 2 Tile Adhesive.

The next step is Picking and Post Goods Issue.

---

## SAP Learning

This step demonstrates how a Sales Order moves into the shipping process in SAP S/4HANA.

The Outbound Delivery acts as the main document for preparing and processing the shipment.

# Enterprise Structure

## What is Enterprise Structure?

Enterprise structure shows how a company is organized inside SAP S/4HANA.

For this project, BuildBond Chemicals Pvt. Ltd. uses the following organizational structure.

## Organizational Units

| Organizational Unit | Code | Meaning in This Project |
|---|---|---|
| Company Code | BB01 | BuildBond Chemicals Pvt. Ltd. |
| Sales Organization | BS01 | India Sales Organization |
| Distribution Channel | 10 | Dealer Sales |
| Division | 01 | Construction Chemicals |
| Plant | BP01 | Ahilyanagar Plant |
| Shipping Point | SP01 | Ahilyanagar Dispatch Point |

## Sales Area

A Sales Area is a combination of:

**Sales Organization + Distribution Channel + Division**

For this project:

**BS01 + 10 + 01 = BuildBond Dealer Sales Area**

## Simple Business Explanation

The India Sales Organization sells construction chemical products to dealers.

The products are supplied from the Ahilyanagar Plant.

The Ahilyanagar Dispatch Point manages the shipping of products to customers.
